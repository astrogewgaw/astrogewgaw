Hey folx! I am **Ujjwal Panda**, an undergraduate from the *Birla Institute of Technology and Science* (*BITS*), Pilani. I am looking for a PhD position in the field of **astrochemistry** for Fall 2020/2021. I also work with **pulsars**! My pipeline has already discovered two previously unknown pulsars, and we are hoping for more as I work on an updated version!

You can find me on [**Twitter**](https://twitter.com/astrogewgaw) and on [**GitHub**](https://github.com/astrogewgaw) as **astrogewgaw**!
